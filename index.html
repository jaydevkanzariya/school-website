Welcome to Rspm Vividhalaxi Sarva Vidhyalaya High School,kadi!

Ours is the Philanthropic institute named as RSPM Mutipurpose Sarva Vidalaya High School kadi . This School has the legacy of about 100 Years. It was starsted in 1919 by late Honorable Chhaganbha and later it was well managed by Late Shri Maneklal Saheb. His visionary approach to Education brought about unprecedented benefits. Following is the general in formation of our Apart Form this, we have Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL) Lab Which is governed by Delhi Central Government . This Lab is equipped With many Scientific instruments to satisfy the curiosity of seience students. We also have the smart rooms for the extra ordinary students to address their curiosity. We also have 1 secondary Lab and 3 Labs of Higher secondary classes .

Vision

A good system of education must instill self –esteem and creativity in children. It must help Natural, fearless, anger free and relaxed mind. We integrate human values with an education based on Pujya Chhaganbha and Pujya Manekdada’s philosophy of compassion and caring and sharing. We spread the education based on the principles of two Mantras, “ Do Good, Goodwill come back to you” (Pujya Chhaganbha) and” Education is the real service” (Pujya manekdada)

Mission

We know that the function of real education is to make the students free. We also try to give the students real education to make them free. Our school strives hard to make the best possible efforts to inculcate strong values combining with academic and extracurricular activities to the children. Our students grow with an optimum blend of excellence in academics and human values. They are shining examples of mental, physical spiritual and ethical values. Love, Truthfulness, Real life, Peace, Non- violence and Patriotism are the values, we are serving to our children through word and deeds and Pranayama.

We give practice to our children to” Love all and Serve all “.We try to make every child a noble citizen. We are eager to teach them faith in self and faith in God and this enables them to overcome any tough situation in their life. So I hope that our noble students can spread the light of true education in the Society. And in conclusion, ‘’ Never underestimate your life. It is the most precious. You never know what talents are hidden within you and what you have to offer to the world ’’

Mr. Kanubhai Patel
